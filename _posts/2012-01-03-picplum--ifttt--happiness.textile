<p>Want to automatically add photos to your Picplum account when you post photos to other services? You can use the service <strong>ifttt</strong> (short for if this then that). It lets you create triggers and then do something when that happens. We have started seeing Picplum users making <strong><a href="http://ifttt.com/recipes/search?q=picplum" title="ifttt Picplum">ifttt “recipes” for Picplum</a> </strong>and other services like Flickr, Tumblr and Instagram.</p>
